Our mission

We exist to provide safe, joyful, and inclusive early childhood experiences—supporting the strengths and needs of every child and family.

Values

  • Family Partnership & Consent
  • Belonging & Dignity
  • Evidence-Based Practice
  • Play, Curiosity, and Joy
  • Safety & Accessibility

Accessibility & Inclusion

We welcome children with intellectual & developmental disabilities (IDD), autism, ADHD, Down syndrome, and other learning and support needs. Our approach is strengths-based and collaborative with families and clinicians as requested.

  • Sensory-friendly design (quiet spaces, visual timers, first-then boards)
  • Communication supports (visuals, AAC welcome)
  • Flexible scheduling and transitions
  • Coordinated SLP/OT visits with family consent
