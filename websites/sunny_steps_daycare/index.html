Why families choose us

Individualized Support

We partner with caregivers to develop personalized supports and routines. We welcome IEPs/IFSPs and collaborate with therapists as families request.

Sensory-Smart Environments

Quiet corners, visual schedules, flexible seating, noise-reducing options, and predictable routines help every child regulate and thrive.

Evidence-Based Practices

We draw from play-based learning, ABA-informed strategies (family-directed), social stories, and strengths-based communication.

Programs at a glance

Early Learners (18–36 mo)

Gentle transitions, parallel play, and caregiver-informed sensory routines.

Explorers (3–4 yrs)

Language play, social-emotional learning, and guided peer interaction.

Pre-K (4–5 yrs)

Pre-literacy, numeracy, and executive-function games in small groups.
