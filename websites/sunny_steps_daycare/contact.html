Policies & Privacy

Health & Safety

Children must meet current immunization requirements per Illinois licensing or provide approved exemptions. Keep children home if fever (100.4°F+), vomiting, diarrhea, or other contagious symptoms; they may return when symptom-free for 24 hours without medication. Medications are administered only with written authorization in labeled containers; emergency care plans (e.g., allergies, seizures, asthma) are maintained with caregiver and clinician input. We follow daily cleaning/disinfection, frequent handwashing, safe sleep, and posted emergency procedures; epinephrine and first-aid supplies are stored and accessible to trained staff.

Privacy

We collect only the information needed to provide care (forms, health needs, schedules, contact details). Records are stored securely and accessed by staff on a need-to-know basis. We share information with therapists or schools only with your written consent; photo/video is opt-in and never used for marketing without permission. We do not sell personal data; electronic submissions are transmitted via Formspree and handled under its policies.

Non-Discrimination

Sunny Steps welcomes all families and does not discriminate on the basis of disability, developmental or learning differences, race, color, national origin, language, sex, gender identity, religion, family structure, or income. We are committed to equitable, accessible services and reasonable accommodations.
